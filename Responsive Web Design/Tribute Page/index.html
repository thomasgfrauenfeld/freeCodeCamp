Freddie Mercury

The music icon who defied the conventions of Rock & Roll with his flamboyance and theatrical style

Here's a timeline of Freddie Mercury's Life:

  • 1946 - Born Farrokh Bulsara in Zanzibar, Tanzania
  • 1954 - Began studying at St. Peter's School in Panchgani, India
  • 1958 - Formed a school band called The Hectics
  • 1963 - Returned to Zanzibar to live with his parents
  • 1964 - Fled to England from Zanzibar with his family to escape the violence of the revolution against the Sultan of Zanzibar
  • 1969 - Graduated with a diploma in graphic art and design from Ealing Art College; joined Liverpool-based band Ibex (later renamed Wreckage); met Mary Austin, with whom he had a long-term relationship
  • 1970 - Joined Brian May and Roger Taylor as the lead singer of their band Smile
  • 1971 - Formed Queen with Brian May, Roger Taylor, and John Deacon; legally changed his surname from Bulsara to Mercury
  • 1976 - Told Mary Austin of his sexuality, ending their relationship
  • 1985 - Began a long-term relationship with Irish-born hairdresser Jim Hutton
  • 1986 - Had his blood tested for HIV
  • 1987 - Was diagnosed with AIDS
  • 1990 - Made his final appearance on stage, where he joined Queen to collect the Brit Award for Outstanding Contribution to British Music
  • 1991 - Died in his home at the age of 45 from bronchial pneumonia resulting from AIDS

"The most important thing is to live a fabulous life. As long as it's fabulous, I don't care how long it is"
